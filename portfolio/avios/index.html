Avios, part of International Airlines Group, run the leading loyalty currency used by British Airways, Iberia, Aer Lingus and other top airlines.

I joined the Collection Tribe as Tech Lead, working on new products to help members earn more Avios on their day-to-day spending.

I worked on a number of projects, including:

  • The relaunch of the British Airways and Aer Lingus eStores, which allows Avios collectors to earn points when they shop online with their favourite retailers (from ASOS to John Lewis). I led a team of developers, shaping the team culture and putting in place best practices in testing, monitoring and continuous integration.
  • An upcoming integration with the challenger bank Monese. I helped to define the Minimum Viable Product (MVP), identify the integration points and support Monese's development team to deliver the initial integration within weeks.
  • A new chatbot to streamline the experience of claiming "Missing Avios"
