At Duffel, I was one of the first engineers helping to build the next generation of travel booking infrastructure. Duffel provides APIs that allow travel companies to search, book, and manage flights from multiple airlines through a single, modern interface.

I joined Duffel as one of the founding team members and helped scale the company from a small startup to processing millions of dollars in bookings for travel companies around the world.

Working primarily in Elixir and Phoenix, I built core platform features, APIs, and integrations that allowed travel companies to access airline inventory and create seamless booking experiences for their customers.

Key contributions included:

  • Building core flight search and booking APIs used by travel companies worldwide
  • Implementing airline integrations to connect with major carriers' booking systems
  • Developing robust payment processing and financial reconciliation systems
  • Creating developer-friendly API documentation and SDKs
  • Scaling the platform to handle millions of search requests and bookings
  • Building internal tools for customer support and operations teams
