At GoCardless, I design and build solutions independently and in small teams in Ruby used to process over $1bn of payments per year.

Over my six years at the company since joining as the fifth employee, I've worked in a variety of roles, from running customer support to kicking off customer success to leading our partnerships team to now being an engineer, on a huge range of projects.

I scope and deliver projects from start to finish, defining requirements and working with users internally and externally to perfect the end product.

Recently, I've worked on:

  • Revamping our developer documentation to make it much easier to get started taking payments via the API (see my blog post)
  • Building a custom workflow for internationalising at scale
  • Refactoring our OAuth implementation for greater maintainability using Coach
  • Implementing a reusable framework for issuing encrypted JSON Web Tokens, used to securely pass information betweeen services
  • Creating an in-product experience to guide users through their first steps of using the service
